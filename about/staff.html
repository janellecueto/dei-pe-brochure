PRINCIPALS

Davis L. Dickerson PE

President

Mr. D. Dickerson is a registered Professional Engineer. He received his BS and MS degrees from the University of Mississippi in 1981 and 1983 respectively.

Prior to joining the DEI team, he was affiliated with Texas Instruments as a Research and Development Engineer. Mr. D. Dickerson joined DEI in 1985, was named to the Board of Directors in 1987, and appointed President in 2012.

His design background includes telecommunication systems, computer, municipal, educational and healthcare projects with particular expertise in data centers and uninterruptible power systems

As a principal of DEI, Mr. D. Dickerson shares in the supervision of all projects and is in responsible charge of project design, coordination, and preparation of construction documents.
