Welcome to Dickerson Engineering, Inc.

DICKERSON ENGINEERING INC. is proud of the reputation we have earned in the electrical engineering field for quality services and client satisfaction. Our dedication to the standards of our profession and to the people we serve shows at every level of our organization. DEI offers complete electrical design services from schematic design through construction administration. To best serve our clients' needs DEI utilizes the latest technology to produce accurate documents with speed and to electronically transfer information to our clients and consultants.

Our objective is to invest in our people and new technology in order to remain a distinguished design team devoted to professionalism, growth, long term client relationships and quality services.

SERVICES

DESIGN DOCUMENTS

We offer a complete package of design services pertinent to the electrical design of each project.

COMMISSIONING

Our Commissioning (Cx) service includes performing design, submittal review, O&M Manual review, development of testing and commissioning processes for the project, as well as documenting the commissioning efforts.

ENERGY CODE COMPLIANCE

We perform energy code studies to verify their compliance and make design suggestions to help bring the project within compliance, if necessary, and in many cases provides recommendations to exceed the requirements
