EMPLOYMENT OPPORTUNITIES

Want to join the DEI team? DEI is continually looking for energetic, motivated staff who seek exciting, challenging projects and opportunities for growth in a team environment.

DEI provides progressive compensation, excellent medical benefits package, paid vacations and sick leave, and an IRA-SEP profit sharing plan. DEI's affirmative action program ensures equal employment opportunities for all persons.

DEI also offers limited internship opportunities to qualified students, in both the electrical engineering and business administration fields.

All applicants must have good communication skills, ability to work in a design team, and the desire to grow with Dickerson Engineering, Inc. If interested in employment with DEI, email, fax or send your resume to us at the addresses below.
